About Us


We are a UK zero waste shop with all of your everyday essentials for sustainable living. Simple, eco swaps anyone can start making today to reduce your impact on our precious environment. Before we started EcoShop, we can remember how good it felt slowly eliminating one plastic product from our home after another. Our biggest drive to make these simple swaps was so we could protect the planet for our kids and for the generations to come, so they could enjoy the great outdoors in the same way we did.
To say that shopping eco friendly products is expensive is a myth, and we want to help disprove this. Start small and simple with what you can afford. The cheapest eco swaps are in the kitchen, so change your cleaning products, dish brushes and clothes first. You will see these don’t cost much, and they will actually save you money in the long run since they are made better and last so much longer.
Eco Friendly shopping has never been easier for all of your favourite green products & zero waste products.
